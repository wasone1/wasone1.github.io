EV Charge Pro

A precision charging calculator for electric vehicles and portable power stations. Estimate charging time, energy usage and total cost based on real-world parameters.

Closed Testing – Coming Soon

What It Calculates

  • Charging time (based on current & target battery level)
  • Total energy added (kWh)
  • Estimated charging cost
  • Remaining battery reserve

Calculation Engine

The algorithm uses manufacturer-based battery capacity ranges, charger power limits, electricity price and charging efficiency to provide realistic and practical estimations.

Charging Modes

  • AC Grid charging
  • Solar input
  • Combined mode
  • Portable station charging
